Instructions for the test

In this test, you will watch a video (~25 seconds) at first.

Then, this video will be divided to 5 clips, and you will watch each of them.

After watching each clip, your job is to label the clip's "importance" in terms of your watching the 25-second long video experience. If a clip is regarded as "important", it means you pay more attention on this clip, AND you really care more about the video quality of this clip compared to those "unimportant" clips.

Please watch all the videos in its entirety and answer each question completely and honestly. You may not be paid if these conditions are not met, for example, give random feedback.

Please take the simple survey before the test.

We use the results to look for trends in responses.
